Food Safety Inspection Checklist

Hygiene

  • Are employees wearing clean uniforms?
  • Are handwashing facilities accessible and stocked?
  • Is there evidence of proper handwashing?

Food Storage

  • Are food items stored at the correct temperature?
  • Are raw and cooked foods stored separately?
  • Are storage areas clean and organized?

Temperature Control

  • Are refrigerators and freezers maintaining the correct temperatures?
  • Are cooked foods held at safe temperatures?

Pest Control

  • Are there any signs of pests?
  • Are pest control measures in place?

Documentation

  • Are food safety records up to date?
  • Are cleaning schedules documented and followed?
